# Password Self-Service Reset

## Prerequisites

Access to either your registered mobile number or personal recovery
email, whichever was configured during onboarding.

## Resolution steps

1. Navigate to the Self-Service Portal login page and select
   **"Forgot Password?"** below the login form.
2. Enter your company email address
   (e.g. `firstname.lastname@company.com`).
3. Select a verification method: SMS code or email link.
4. Enter the 6-digit code received, or select the link in your email
   (valid for 15 minutes).
5. Create a new password meeting the complexity policy:
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, one special character
   - Must not match any of the last 5 passwords used
6. Confirm the new password. All active sessions are terminated for
   security — re-authenticate with the new password.

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| "Account not found" | Login attempted with username instead of full email | Use the complete company email address |
| Verification code not received | Registered contact details are outdated | Verify current contact details with the Service Desk |
| Account locked after repeated attempts | Lockout threshold exceeded | Wait 15 minutes for automatic unlock, or request an immediate unlock |

## When to contact the Service Desk instead

Escalate directly if: the account status shows Disabled rather than
Locked, registered phone/email access has been lost, or more than two
lockouts have occurred within a single day (a potential indicator of
unauthorized access attempts).
