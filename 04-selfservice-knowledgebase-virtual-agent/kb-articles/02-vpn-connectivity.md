# Connecting to the Company VPN

**Category:** Network Connectivity
**Audience:** Remote/hybrid employees
**Estimated time:** 5 minutes

## Steps

1. Open the VPN client (GlobalProtect / AnyConnect — check with IT for
   your company's client) from your Start Menu or Applications folder.
2. Enter the VPN gateway address if not pre-filled: `vpn.company.com`.
3. Log in with your company username and password.
4. Approve the MFA push notification on your registered device.
5. Wait for the client to show "Connected" — this can take 10–30 seconds.

## Common issues

| Symptom | Likely cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| "Gateway unreachable" | Local internet issue, not VPN | Confirm you can browse the open internet first |
| Connects then drops repeatedly | Weak Wi-Fi signal or ISP instability | Switch to a wired connection or move closer to the router |
| MFA push never arrives | Notification permissions off on phone | Check the authenticator app has notifications enabled |
| "Certificate error" | Client is outdated | Reinstall the latest VPN client from the Software Center |

## When to contact the Service Desk instead

If you've confirmed your internet connection works but the VPN client
itself won't launch, shows a licensing error, or you were recently
offboarded/reassigned and lost access unexpectedly.
