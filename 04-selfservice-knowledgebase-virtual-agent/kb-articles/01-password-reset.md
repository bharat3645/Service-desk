# How to Reset Your Password

**Category:** Password / Account Access
**Audience:** All employees
**Estimated time:** 3 minutes

## Before you start

You'll need access to either your registered mobile number or personal
recovery email — whichever you set up during onboarding.

## Steps

1. Go to the Self-Service Portal login page and click **"Forgot Password?"**
   below the login form.
2. Enter your company email address (e.g. `firstname.lastname@company.com`).
3. Choose your verification method: SMS code or email link.
4. Enter the 6-digit code sent to you, or click the link in your email
   (valid for 15 minutes).
5. Create a new password meeting the complexity policy:
   - At least 12 characters
   - One uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, one special character
   - Cannot reuse your last 5 passwords
6. Confirm the new password. You'll be logged out of all active sessions
   for security — log back in with your new password.

## If it doesn't work

- **"Account not found"**: Confirm you're using your full company email,
  not just your username.
- **No SMS/email received**: Check your registered contact details are
  current. If unsure, contact the Service Desk to verify what's on file.
- **Account locked after too many attempts**: Wait 15 minutes for the
  automatic unlock, or contact the Service Desk for an immediate unlock.

## When to contact the Service Desk instead

Contact us directly if: your account shows as disabled (not locked), you
no longer have access to your registered phone/email, or you've been
locked out more than twice in one day (may indicate a security issue).
